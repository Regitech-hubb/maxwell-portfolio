About

Site Supervisor
who Delivers Quality

📍 Rarieda, Siaya County, Kenya

I am a qualified Building Technician and Site Supervisor based in Rarieda, Siaya County. With 7+ projects successfully completed across residential and institutional builds, I deliver quality construction through proper supervision, precise planning, and attention to detail.

From setting out foundations to final finishing works, I ensure every phase meets the required standards — because one wrong measurement is a costly mistake that can never be undone.

What I Do

Core Skills & Expertise

📐
Site Setting Out
Precise setting out of building lines, levels, and grids ensuring correct positioning of all structural elements from day one.
🏗️
Foundation Works
Supervision of excavation, DPC, DPM, blinding, and strip foundations ensuring structural integrity at every level.
🪨
Concrete Works
Slab casting, beam and column supervision, steel fixing, formwork, and concrete quality control to specification.
🏠
Roofing & Finishing
Roof structure supervision, iron sheet installation, plastering, floor screeding, tiling, and all finishing trades.
🏫
Institutional Projects
Experienced in government and CDF-funded school projects including classroom blocks and administration buildings.
📋
Site Management
Full site management, labour supervision, materials procurement coordination, and quality control reporting.
