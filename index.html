How a site runs under my supervision

Four phases. Nothing skipped.

01 — Foundation

Setting out & groundwork

Site clearing, setting out, excavation levels, and foundation reinforcement checked against structural drawings before any concrete is poured.

02 — Structure

Walling, columns & roofing

Material quality checks, formwork inspection, curing schedules, and roofing structure supervision — this is where most defects start if no one is watching.

03 — Finishing

Plastering, fittings & services

Coordination between plumbing, electrical, and finishing trades so nothing gets sealed in before it's inspected.

04 — Handover

Snag list & sign-off

A walk-through snag list, defect close-out, and a documented handover so the client knows exactly what they're taking possession of.
